Before you begin

How this course is built to actually stick

You learn a tool by doing one small, real task and getting quick feedback, not by reading about it. Every lesson follows the same short rhythm. Do one per sitting; spacing them out helps them stick.

1

Watch & Read

A short video plus one quick article frame the idea. Roughly 15 minutes of input, no jargon.

2

Try it now

You complete one real Brighton-relevant task using a free tool. This is where the learning happens.

3

Quiz & carry forward

A 4-question check locks in the key points, then you see how it feeds the next lesson.

Why this order? Lesson 2 (prompting) is the master skill; Lessons 3–5 apply it; Lesson 6 covers responsible use. Follow the sequence your first time through.
